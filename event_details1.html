Aquarium Visit for Kids

May, 2017

In May of 2017, IMYA organized a retreat to the Vancouver Aquarium, where all members had the opportunity to explore, learn, and sleep over at the Aquarium. This visit began the long lasting friendships that would bond the IMYA members together as a team for years to come.

Who we are

Support Chinese Immigrants

Problem: Chinese immigrants often struggle in bridging the gap between Chinese and Canadian culture. Zong from the University of Saskatchewan observes that “about 34% of those immigrants experienced difficulties in adapting to Western culture.” We want to provide them with a sense of belonging by establishing a multicultural community.

Our Mission: We provide and organise engaging and motivating community and charity activities for teenagers and parents to improve their teamwork, organisation, and leadership skills. We foster youth philanthropy while spreading multiculturalism to a broader and more diverse group of audience, combining Eastern and Western cultures.

Our Vision: We hope to create a community for Chinese immigrants through events and initiatives, such as Spring Festival Galas and traditional Chinese celebrations, while fundraising an impactful amount of money for local charities. In the future, we aim to expand our operations beyond British Columbia to reach other communities in need.

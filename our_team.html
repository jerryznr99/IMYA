Jason Song
Secretary

Jason Song is a grade 10 student attending Mulgrave school, and he is a proud member of IMYA. He loves helping out his community, biking with his friends, and debating. Throughout the years as a member of IMYA, he has been able to coach new immigrant students and teach them the fundamentals of speech. He has also been fortunate enough to participate in the Chinese new year festivals through comedy. Through IMYA, he connected to his heritage. He was fortunate enough to be able to raise donations towards the most vulnerable in hospitals. Each innovative project completed with the IMYA allowed him to feel touched and blessed at the amount of change he is able to create.
